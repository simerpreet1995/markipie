What is Search Engine Optimization?

In simple words, Search Engine Optimization or SEO is optimizing a web page in a way that it appears at the top of the search engine result pages (SERPs) for relevant keywords. The primary objective of SEO is to bring more organic traffic to a website through various SEO activities. When someone searches for a specific keyword or phrase on a search engine, the goal of SEO is to have your website appear as high as possible in the search results. The higher your website's ranking, the more likely it is to attract clicks and visits from users searching for information, products, or services related to your industry.
